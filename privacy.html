Privacy policy.

What we collect, what we don't, and how we handle your information. The short version: we keep it simple and respect your data.

What we collect

When you use the contact form on this site, we collect the information you provide: your name, business name, phone number (optional), and your message. That's it. We don't collect anything else.

How it's processed

Contact form submissions are sent through FormSubmit.co directly to our email inbox. We don't store your information in a database. Once the message lands in email, that's where it lives.

No tracking

This site does not use cookies, analytics scripts, or tracking pixels. No Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no session recording. When you visit this site, we have no idea you were here unless you reach out to us directly.

No third-party sharing

We don't sell, trade, or share your information with anyone. Your contact details are used for one thing: responding to your inquiry. That's it.

Your rights

If you've reached out to us and want your information deleted, just ask. We'll remove any emails and records associated with your inquiry. No forms to fill out, no hoops to jump through.
